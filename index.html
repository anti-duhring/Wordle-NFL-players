Read the player stats above the keyboard and try to find who that player is. You have 5 shots.

Remeber the space between the name and last name. The answer is always the name + last name. Ex: 'Tom brady'.

Every day is a differente player.

Type the letters inside the box pressing the keys on your computer or using the virtual keyboard. When you finish press Enter.

If the box becomes green it means that you got the letter on the right spot, if becomes yellow it means that you got the letter but it is on the wrong spot, and if becomes red it means that you don't get the letter at all.
